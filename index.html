About Me

Hi there! I'm Raja, a second-year electronics and communication engineering with specialization in vlsi and embedded systems student at IIIT Manipur. I was curious about how websites work, and now I'm building them myself using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I'm learning even more about making great websites. When I'm not coding, I'm writing. I create content for the IIITians Network, connecting students from all IIITs in India.
